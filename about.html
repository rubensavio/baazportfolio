Founding conviction

"Beautiful engineering and fast delivery aren't trade-offs — they're the same discipline, practiced well."

We started Baaz in 2018 with a specific frustration: the best software we'd ever seen came from small, obsessed teams — not large agencies with layer-cake processes and account managers in the room. The belief hasn't changed.

What started as a two-person studio has shipped over a hundred products across ten industries. FinTech platforms, construction field apps, healthcare ERPs, AI document systems. Half the work that comes to us starts as a rescue — products that stalled under other vendors, codebases that became unmaintainable, things that shipped but never took off.

We take that as a compliment. It means people know where to go when it needs to be done right.
